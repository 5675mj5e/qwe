Get rid of mold & mildew on the walls with vinegar and baking soda. Mold is unpleasant, both by appearance and by the harmful effects it has on our health. Baking soda hacks Baking soda can work on its own as a cleaner with no need for vinegar. Lynsey recommends sprinkling it on mattresses, shoes. Sprinkle on enough baking soda to cover the bottom of the pan, and then using a spray bottle or a dropper, add enough vinegar to make it fizz.
